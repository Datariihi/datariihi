Welcome to Datariihi!

Datariihi is a Seinäjoki-based computer service company.

The name: how do you translate something that is... untranslatable? Data is data and riihi is a Finnish word for a building of olden days that was used to dry and harvest grain. A kind of drying barn.

We are available where ever you need us. At your place, on the road, remote controlling your computer or at our office in Pohja neighborhood of Seinäjoki.

We welcome anyone who needs help with any kind of information technology and whom we think we are able to help somehow.

Contact us, so we can have a chat and see how we could be of service :)

English and Swedish language service available!
